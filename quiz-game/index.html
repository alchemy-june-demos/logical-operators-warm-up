Alchemy Quiz Game!

Rules

  • Each team member must answer one question
  • If you get the answer right on your own, you get 2 points for your team.
  • You may "phone a friend" (i.e. ask your teammates) and get 1 point for your team.
  • If you get it wrong, the opposite team can "steal" for 1 point but if you get it wrong on the steal you lose a point.
  • You must decide before the question is revealed which team member is going to be answering the question.
